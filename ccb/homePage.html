Community Connections Botswana (CCB) is a Non Governmental Organisation operating a drop in rehabilitation centre for people with disabilities (PWDs). The drop centre provides day care services.

CCB exist to:

  • We offer services that enable PWDs to increase and maintain their level of independence and live independently as they choose.
  • Help people with disabilities accessing assistive technology, aids and adaptation to support independence, safety and manage risks.
  • Ensure that people with disabilities are able to access activities appropriate to their needs.
  • Help PWDs building local social and support networks.
  • Support PWDs play a valued role in their community.
  • Help PWDs to have opportunities to meet friends, and make new friends.
